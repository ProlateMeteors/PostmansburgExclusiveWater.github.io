Who We Are?

Postmansburg Exclusive Water Pty Ltd, founded in 2020 by Tshepang Linda-latifa Moremedi, is a leading distributor of bottled still water based in Postmansburg, Northern Cape, South Africa. The company is committed to providing the people of Postmansburg and the wider South African market with access to clean and refreshing bottled still water. In addition to its core product, the company offers a range of refreshing and nutritious beverage options that cater to consumers seeking healthier choices in their beverages.

We are committed to staying up-to-date with the latest health trends and providing exceptional customer service to ensure our customers feel their best.

Mission and Vision

Mission

Our mission at PMG Exclusive Water is to provide our customers with the highest quality water that quenches their thirst and enhances their well-being. We strive to be a leader in the bottled water industry by upholding the highest standards of purity, taste, and sustainability.

Vision

Our vision is to become the preferred choice for premium bottled water in South Africa and beyond, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and environmental responsibility. We aim to inspire healthier lifestyles through the simple act of drinking water.
